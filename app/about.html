01About Us

Sorgente Group of America Corporation is the holding of Sorgente Group which assembles the Group’s various US real estate, finance, and development initiatives. Led by Veronica Mainetti, the group continues to expand its US portfolio with key acquisitions in California and New York, most notably the iconic Flatiron building. In 2010,  Sorgente Group of America lauched itss development arm and has restored some of New York’s most iconic buildings for residential use, preserving the past while embracing future generations.

02What We Do

Operating mainly in the US, Sorgente Group of America focus is to acquire historic buildings and to restore them to residential grandeur in a distinctly contemporary way.

Typically, 80% of materials are part of the existing structure, which are restored and reclaimed; the rest of the finishes in the buildings are locally sourced.

03La Nostra Filosofia

Over the years, Sorgente Group of America has had the privilege of investing in some of the nation’s most iconic buildings. Selecting buildings that have historic and architectural relevance has been the core of our philosophy. We feel that preserving and restoring what has already been built is a sustainable, responsible use of resources. Restoration is a value that has the ability to better, not just the buildings, but our neighborhood, our company, the economy as a whole and, ultimately, our environment.

We take immense enjoyment out of being able to breathe new life into these buildings that, in a way, reinforce much of what we love about this country. It is so incredibly rewarding to be able to save their embodied energy, which together with their cultural significance will only increase with the passage of time.
I believe that the soul in these great buildings is simply unfathomable. It is this notion, together with the acknowledgement that we will always succeed by selecting such landmark properties, that fuels my passion to continue on this journey.

- Sincerely, Veronica Mainetti.

VERONICA MAINETTI
President of Sorgente Group of America and CEO of Sorgente Asset Management

After specializing in Design at the European Institute of Design in Rome, Veronica Mainetti came to New York to pursue a masters in Design at the Parsons Institute. President of Sorgente Group of America, and Managing Director of the Michelangelo Real Estate Corporation, the company through which Sorgente Group conducted investment transactions in the United States for an overall value of 2 billion dollars. She is CEO of Sorgente Asset Management, a company established in 2004 in New York, which is active in real estate management, advisory and brokerage. She is also board member of the Michelangelo Flatiron Building Investment and the Sorgente Group Foundation in the US.

VALTER MAINETTI
Director of Sorgente Group of America

From the mid '70's to the year 1999 he managed the group of Companies of which his family had control. He has worked both in Italy and abroad, operating as director in charge for both real estate and finance operations. Since 1987 he has led the Parent Company of the real estate sector, "SAGEFIM PARTECIPAZIONI S.p.A. (now known as TIBERIADE – Servizi Finanziari S.p.A.). As of the year 1999, he is in involved in real estate funds and is Chief Executive Officer of SORGENTE SGR S.p.A. and SORGENTE GROUP S.p.A. The latter operates with three holding companies: Sorgente Group SpA (Italy), Sorgente Group of America Corp. (USA) and Sorgente Group International Holding Ltd. (UK). The real estate, financial and real estate service companies operating around the world amount to more than 60. Since the 2001 the Group has launched 34 real estate funds. He is President of the Fondazione Sorgente Group, Istituzione per l'Arte e la Cultura and Professor at the Università degli Studi di Parma, Facoltà di Economia, (University of Parma, School of Economics) for the Degree Specialized in Finance and Risk Management.
